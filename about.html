About Me

Pencil sketching, which was a hobby since my toddler days, has now evolved into a passion in me and elevated me to this level. I always have an admiration for art in its varied forms. However, I am attracted towards pencil drawings mostly. Of late, I have started devoting myself to Painting and Digital Medium.

I'm a self - taught product in the field of art and I have worked a lot to steal your precious time to travel through the avenues of these artworks. I utilize all the technical skills I have developed to tell a story or evoke an emotion. I feel I've successfully communicated with someone when they identify themselves with the essence of a particular piece, and it brings about new thoughts or feelings.

And now, Art has become a passion and an inevitable component of my life.I see art in every unique object, thing or an idea.

-Gsp Prabhu
